Deep Mapping Workshop
Doing STS What could it mean to think with place, to feel the city? This workshop will guide participants into dialogue with the urban surround through deep mapping, a capacious practice of situated, embodied inhabitation. Disorient the familiar. Interfere with legible representations and intelligible forms rendered down from above by flat maps by walking alleyways, sitting with parking lots, and finding stories in the wor(l)ds of dumpsters. You are invited to begin a praxis. Come attune to the rhythm of an intersection; be grasped; surrender yourself to the tug and pull of the city’s affective contours… Society for Social Studies of Science (4s) 2023 Conference Diffractive mapping spatial analysis: how data comes to matter through empirical formations
