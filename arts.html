I'm an artist-crafter local to Vancouver. If you'd like to purchase/exchange for my work, please email me at either overtherainbowarts@gmail.com or lilydemet.proton.me with your name, contact info, and request. Happy to mail my work for cost of shipping or exchange IRL in the city. I am open to select commissions - please reach out if you have something in mind. At a time when art can feel like a luxury, I maintain accessible prices by working with gleaned and appropriated materials. My items are therefore imperfect and bear the marks of the machines with whom I collaborated in the making.
